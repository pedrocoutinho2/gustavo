WHO AM I?

The beginning

My name is Gustavo Coutinho and I’m 19 years old. I live in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, and I've been studyingGraphic design for a year now. Before my education, I’ve always been creating, but not just photo-editing. Imade draw's, video’s, photo’s, all kinds of stuff.

You see, I’ve always been busy doing creative stuff or playing games in my free time. However, I didn’t know Photoshop. Or… well, I knew it, I just didn’t like it all that much. However, one year ago I started my study and got pushed to work with Adobe Photoshop. After some weeks of doing random stuff and school work, I absolutely fell in love with it. Sadly, they didn’t teach me a lot about Photoshop. That’s why I started learning myself, just by using it a LOT! I opened a new Instagram account, started as an intern, and then all this happened!

What I do?

I'm a Graphic Designer and Digital Artist who makes draws as a hobby. During my days I've been working and learning a lot to get what I want!

My destination

I'm looking for the perfect option, but didn't find it yet. What I want is to be recognized, to be known as a great designer/artist, I want to work in big projects and exploring more and more all of this beautiful creativity world.
